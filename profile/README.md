## Kenotic Labs

**The continuity layer for AI systems.**

Most AI today is intelligent per session. Continuity is what makes AI coherent across life.

We build the infrastructure layer between AI interaction and AI relationship — the logic that determines what should persist, in what form, what has changed, what still matters, and how to reconstruct it when needed.

Continuity is not memory. Memory stores the past. Continuity keeps the right parts alive in the present.

### What We Publish

- **[ATANT](https://github.com/Kenotic-Labs/ATANT)** — An open evaluation framework for testing AI continuity. 7 required properties, 10 checkpoints, 250 narrative tests, 1,835 verification questions. No LLM in the evaluation loop.

### The 7 Properties of Continuity

Persistence beyond session. Update handling. Temporal ordering. Disambiguation. Reconstruction. Model independence. Operational usefulness.

Any system claiming continuity must satisfy all seven.
